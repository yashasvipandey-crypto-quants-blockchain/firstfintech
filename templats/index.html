Process

How FIDUCIA Works

01

Collect Data

Historical OHLCV data and available news headlines are requested through yfinance for the selected supported ticker.

02

Analyse Signals

RSI, MACD, SMA, volatility, volume ratio, ADX, ATR, Bollinger Band width, momentum, and headline sentiment are used as signal inputs.

03

Generate Trust Score

The backend combines model confidence, news sentiment, volatility, trend strength, and feature health into a single Trust Score.
